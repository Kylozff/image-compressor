📱 About ImageCompress Pro

ImageCompress Pro is a free online image compression tool that helps you reduce image file sizes by up to 90% without losing quality.

Built by Prince Prajapat, our mission is to provide a simple, secure, and private way to compress images. Unlike other tools, your files never leave your device - all processing happens directly in your browser.

✨ Features

🔒 100% Private

No uploads - everything stays on your device.

⚡ Instant Compression

Compress images in seconds, right in your browser.

📊 Before/After View

See the difference side by side.

🎨 All Formats

JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF supported.

📱 Mobile Friendly

Works on all devices - phone, tablet, desktop.

🆓 100% Free

No limits, no premium plans, no signup.

💡 Why Free?

We believe essential tools should be accessible to everyone. ImageCompress Pro is supported by minimal, non-intrusive advertising to keep the tool free for all users worldwide.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies. Hosted on GitHub Pages with a custom domain. Open source under MIT License.
