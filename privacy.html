🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

🛡️ TL;DR: Your images NEVER leave your device. We can't see them, access them, or store them. Everything happens in your browser.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

ImageCompress Pro processes all images directly in your browser. We do NOT upload, store, or have any access to your images or files. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed.

2. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. No names, emails, passwords, or any personal data. All image processing happens locally on your device. No files are ever transmitted to any server.

3. How Compression Works

  • You select images on your device
  • JavaScript loads them into your browser memory
  • HTML5 Canvas processes the compression
  • Compressed files are downloaded directly to you
  • Nothing is ever sent anywhere

4. Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can disable cookies in your browser settings anytime.

Learn more: Google's Advertising Policies

5. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense: Displays advertisements on our site. Google uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads. These cookies help show relevant ads but do not identify you personally.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at: Google Ads Settings

6. Data Security

Since all processing is local, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your files. Your images remain on your device at all times.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tool does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
